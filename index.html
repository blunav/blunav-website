WHAT WE DO

At Blunav, we create intelligent airport systems and services to enable safe, predictable and efficient airport operations.

WHO WE ARE

Blunav is an Airports Systems company incubated at IIT Madras, India. We work on Air Traffic Control & Communication, Navigation and Surveillance (ATM/ CNS) technologies to streamline airport processes, and aid airport to operate safely and efficiently.

COMPANY NEWS

AMCOMS Milestone-1 completed at Chennai International Airport

APRIL 2022

As a part of the "Airside Movement & Compliance Monitoring System (AMCOMS)" contract with Airports of India at Chennai International Airport, the Blunav has successfully completed Milestone-1 as per the Detailed Project Report. The Project Manager for AMCOMS - Shri. V. Muruganandam, GM(CNS), has awarded the Completion Certificate for the work carried out after due verification of deliverables.

Drone based PAPI inspection trial at Tambaram Airforce station

FEB 2022

The NEXIS programme in development at Blunav has been marked with a major milestone - the indigenous drone system developed by Blunav has successfully trailed its PAPI inspection system at Tambaram Air force Station with support from Indian Air force.

Blunav awarded contract by Airports Authority of India for Chennai Airport

FEB 2021

Blunav was selected from over 1400 companies, as a part of the "Innovate for Airports" programme held by Airports Authority of India in Delhi. After interactions with the concerned directorates, Blunav has been awarded a contract for Proof-of-concept deployment of "Airside Movement & Compliance Monitoring System (AMCOMS)" at Chennai International Airport. A joint memorandum has also been signed on this regard.
