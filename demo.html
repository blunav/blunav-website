    Aerios Flight
    Foundation
    The operational core. Flight scheduling, the AODB, and resource allocation across every gate, counter, parking stand, and baggage belt. Every other product line in the suite extends from this layer.
    AODB
    Real-time central data hub for all flight operations across the airport.
    Flight Schedule
    Manage scheduled, planned, and actual flight movements.
    Resource Management
    Allocate gates, counters, stands, and belts with built-in conflict checks.
    Stand Allocation
    Optimise stand assignment and reassignment in real time.
    Counter Management
    Open, close, and assign check-in counters by airline and flight.
    Baggage Belts
    Allocate and track baggage claim belts for arriving flights.
    Turnaround
    Track and optimise aircraft turnaround time.
    Airport CDM
    A-CDM milestones, predictability, and collaborative ops.
